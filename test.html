Support, Hope, Love

We are Colorado's leading provider of early intervention vision services.
Founded in 2016, A Shared Vision is dedicated to serving families with very young children who are blind, deaf-blind, or visually impaired.  In 2020, we supported 211 children and their families, providing over 2,100 in-home education services.

This level of service is made possible through your support and partnerships with Colorado's local Community Centered Boards (CCBs) in 55 counties representing 83% of Colorado’s population.

We're Hiring!

Do you love working with babies and toddlers? Are you interested in supporting little ones with blindness or visual impairments? We’re looking for collaborative, sensitive, licensed vision teachers who are interested in a flexible schedule. We will support the training and mentoring you will need to succeed in early intervention – it is different providing services to caregivers, rather than students! If interested, please send an email to HR@asharedvision.org. Include a short paragraph of why you’re interested in supporting families with children, birth to age three. Please attach your resume, as well.
